# LaserCrafter87
![LaserCrafter87](https://lasercrafter.github.io/images/Capture.PNG)

LaserCrafter87 is a TKL PCB for custom TKL keyboards that use the standard a.87 spacing as found in most custom TKL keyboards, but unlike the a.87 standard, this PCB has its USB connector on the bottom. This PCB features Tsangan (7u) and normal 6.25u bottom row support with both north facing spacebar stabilizer wire orientation along with south facing spacebar stabilizer wire orientation, Caps lock and scroll lock indicator LED support, and RGB underglow.
